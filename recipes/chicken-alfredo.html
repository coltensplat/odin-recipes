Chicken Alfredo recipe

Watch out, alfredo incoming

Saucy...

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Oil
  • Seasonings
  • Aromatics
  • Cream
  • Cheese
  • Pasta

Sauce datshit up

  1. Prep da chicken.... Slice each chicken breast half widthwise to create thin cutlets.
  2. Season da chicken..... Season both sides of the chicken cutlets with salt and pepper.
  3. Sear da chicken..... When you're ready to cook, heat olive oil in a large rondeau pan or deep skillet over high heat until the oil starts to smoke. (smokeyyy...)
  4. Sauteee da aromatics.... In the same pan, add more olive oil, along with the minced garlic and shallots (what, we're using shallots now?), and saute them until they're fragrant.
  5. Make da sauce..... Pour the heavy cream into the pan and stir in the parmesan until the sauce thickens. sauceyyy...
  6. Cook da noods.... Cook it in a large pot of boiling salted water until it's a dentist.
  7. Combine and tossss... Dump the cooked pasta into the pan with the alfredo sauce and toss it all together until every strand is coated. (saucyyyyy)
  8. Slice da chicken..... Slice the seared chicken, and plate it over the pasta that is now covered in alfredo sauce. The chicken will also become covered by the alfredo sauce. Enjoy.

Source: "Chicken Alfredo", Chef Billy Parisi, Chef Billy Parisi
