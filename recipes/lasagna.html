Lasagna Recipe

You too can make lasagna that looks this perfect!

Ingredients

  • Butter for greasing
  • 2 1/2 cups Lady Caterina's Ragu
  • 1 pound fresh mozzarella torn into 1-inch pieces
  • A brick to chop down
  • 3 hard-cooked eggs finely chopped
  • 2 cups bechamel
  • 2 cups finely grated Parmiagiano-Reggiano
  • Some foreign bullshit, the noodle shit; cut that shit into 4 sheets measuring the size of your baking dish nigga, or about a pound of store-bought pasta sheets.

Follow these steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Spoon a half cup layer of ragu on the bottom of the baking dish Place a pasta sheet on top. There's no need to cook the pasta in advance. It will cook in the juices of the ragu. Spoon anotehr half cup of ragu over the pasta. Distribute a quater of the mozzarella, quater of the chopped eggs, half cup of bechamel, and half cup of Parmiagiano-Reggiano evenly over the tray. Repeat the pasta, ragu, mozzarella, egg, bechamel, and Parmiagiano-Reggiano layering three more times. Layer the remaining ragu, mozzarella, bechamel and Parmiagiano-Reggiano over the final pasta layer.
  3. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 30 minutes nigga. Uncover the baking dish, then continue baking until the edges of the pasta are curled up and browned, 30 to 40 more minutes.
  4. Allow the lasanga to rest for about 30 minutes before serving in slices
